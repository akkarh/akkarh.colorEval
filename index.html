Website Colorblind Accessability Checker

Want to figure out if your website or another website on the web is accessable to color blind populations? Provide the url and we will give you analysis of whether or not your website is accessable.

Website made by Nicole Riley, Sai Harshita Neti, Lior Levi, and Harshitha Akkaraju for SWE hacks 2018 at University of Washingtion
