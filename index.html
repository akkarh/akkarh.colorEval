colorEval

Determining Colorblind Accessibility of Web Pages

Want to figure out if your website or another website on the web is accessible to color blind populations? Provide the url and we will give you an analysis of whether or not your website is accessible under the W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines.

This application looks at two different levels to determine accessibility: level AA (which requires a contrast ratio greater than or equal to 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text) and level AAA (which requires a contrast ratio greater than or equal to 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text)
